Music from the 1900's
Back in the 1900's, they used to use music as a background noise on CD players, or tunes from a piano when entertaining their guests. Over time, music became more popular because people started downloading it, or making money from the music they produced. Producing music became more of an interest to people, and after a while it was something leading to an ongoing career. Music has now advanced to higher technology, improving the studios, multitrack records, auto-tune, digital software and more. There's a lot more on offer when recording music and higher quality equipment used to make the music that been release nowadays. The technology now allows artists to incorperate effects and designs into their music, boosting certain sounds or increasing basses to make their music more enjoyble
